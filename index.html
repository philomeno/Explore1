I love the Camino de Santiago

The Camino de Santiago is long distance pilgrimage hike through Spain to the city of Santiago de Compestela in north west Spain.

How long is it?

It is 800 kilometres from the French Pyrenees to Santiago and the main trail starts in the village of Saint jean Pied de Port.




There are also other routes from all over Spain and all over Europe, though must of the European routes meet at Saint Jean Pied de Port.
